## 2. Company Grant

For each Contribution made by an Authorised Contributor, the Company
grants to the Maintainer and to every recipient of the Project the same
copyright licence, patent licence, and relicense right as described in
[`CLA.md §§ 2–3`](CLA.md), as if the Company were the individual
Contributor.

This grant applies regardless of whether the Contribution was made
during working hours, using Company equipment, or in connection with
Company business — the purpose of this clause is to confirm that the
Company waives any IP claim it might otherwise have over such
Contributions.
